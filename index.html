A Good Year (2006)

is a charming romantic comedy-drama directed by Ridley Scott and starring Russell Crowe and Marion Cotillard. The story follows a successful but overworked London investment banker who inherits his uncle's vineyard in Provence, France. What begins as a quick trip to sell the property becomes a life-changing journey of self-discovery, love, and learning to appreciate life's simple pleasures. With breathtaking countryside scenery, heartfelt performances, and a warm, uplifting atmosphere, A Good Year is a feel-good film about finding happiness where you least expect it.
